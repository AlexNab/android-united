About us

Android United is a friendly Slack community of professional Android developers.
At the moment we unite more than 1100 people from all over the world. We share our experience, knowledge and relevant news about Android and mobile. We help each other.

Code of conduct

We strongly encourage politeness, honesty and professionalism in communication.
We do not tolerate flood, spam, harassment and other nonsense. Those who violate the rules will be subject to being expelled from the community.
